Frequently Asked Questions

How do I commission a custom calligraphy piece?

Simply fill out our contact form with details about your project (occasion, preferred style, size, etc.), and we'll respond within 48 hours with a quote and timeline. We work closely with clients to ensure the final piece exceeds expectations.

What types of workshops do you offer?

We offer workshops for all skill levels - from beginner's introduction to advanced techniques. Popular options include modern calligraphy, traditional scripts, brush lettering, and wedding calligraphy. Private group workshops are also available.

Can you create digital calligraphy files?

Yes! We provide high-resolution digital files suitable for printing, invitations, logos, and other digital uses. We can work with your designer or provide complete design services.

What materials do you use for your work?

We use premium materials including archival inks, 24k gold leaf, handmade papers, and professional-grade nibs. For beginners, we recommend our starter kits which include all essential tools.
